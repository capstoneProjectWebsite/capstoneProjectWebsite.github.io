Humber College requires all Information Technology students to complete a semester-long, industry-sponsored Capstone Project, and we are on the hunt for the perfect project.

Humber College


About: The Humber community has 33,000 full-time and 23,000 part-time and continuing education students across three campuses.Humber professors bring a wealth of relevant industry experience to the classroom. Class sizes are small, and simulation labs and work placements bridge the gap between classroom and industry.


Applied Science and Technology Capstone: In the final semester of Information Technology Solutions, students participate in an industry project supervised by one of the Humber industrial partners which helps students apply and enhance their IT, teamwork, interpersonal and project management skills.

Moreover, through a range of capstone showcasing events and employer engagement workshops, students will have a chance to showcase their portfolios and capstone project to employers and practise how to reach prospective employers in today's competitive job market.
